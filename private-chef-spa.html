Questions & Answers

Private Chef FAQ

How much does a private chef in Bansko cost?
A private chef in Bansko starts from €180 per evening. This includes market shopping, preparation, cooking, full table service and clean-up. Price varies by number of guests and menu complexity. Contact us via WhatsApp for a personalised quote.
What is included in the private chef service?
Everything. Your chef arrives 2-3 hours before dinner, shops for the freshest local ingredients, prepares your chosen menu in your kitchen, serves the full meal with wine pairings if requested, and leaves the kitchen spotless. You simply sit down and enjoy.
How far in advance should I book a private chef in Bansko?
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for menu curation and ingredient sourcing. For peak ski season (December–March) and summer, 1 week in advance is ideal. Last-minute requests are handled where possible via WhatsApp.
Can I have a private chef in my Bansko chalet or apartment?
Yes. Our service is fully in-residence — we come to your chalet, apartment or villa. There is no minimum kitchen requirement. Your chef brings all necessary equipment if needed.
Do you offer SPA and wellness services in Bansko?
Yes. We arrange in-residence SPA treatments including massage, facial and body treatments, yoga instruction and full wellness retreats — all in the privacy of your accommodation. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss availability.
Can a private chef cook for a large dinner party?
Yes. We cater for intimate dinners of 2 guests to large dinner parties of 12+. For larger groups, we bring an additional sous-chef. Please mention your guest count when booking.
