Questions & Answers

VIP Transfer FAQ

How much does a VIP transfer from Sofia Airport to Bansko cost?
A luxury VIP transfer from Sofia Airport (SOF) to Bansko starts from €149 one way. This includes meet-and-greet at arrivals, luggage handling, multilingual driver and complimentary refreshments. Contact us via WhatsApp for exact pricing based on group size and vehicle preference.
What makes Bansko Concierge VIP different from a regular taxi?
Bansko Concierge VIP is not a regular taxi or shared shuttle. Your driver meets you at arrivals with a name board, handles all luggage, speaks English, and the vehicle is a luxury Mercedes. You can make on-demand changes, request stops and contact your driver directly. One number, zero waiting.
How long does the transfer from Sofia Airport to Bansko take?
The drive from Sofia Airport to Bansko takes approximately 1 hour 45 minutes to 2 hours, depending on traffic. The route follows the A3 motorway through the scenic Struma Valley.
Can I book a luxury transfer from Bansko to Sofia Airport?
Yes. We operate both directions — Sofia Airport to Bansko and Bansko to Sofia Airport. We also offer transfers to Plovdiv Airport, Athens, Thessaloniki and all destinations in our 8-country Balkan corridor.
What vehicles are available for the VIP transfer?
Luxury sedans (Mercedes E-Class or equivalent) for 1-3 passengers, and luxury minivans (Mercedes V-Class or equivalent) for groups of 4-7 passengers. All vehicles are clean, climate-controlled and equipped with Wi-Fi on request.
How do I book a luxury transfer to Bansko?
The easiest way is via WhatsApp at +359 895 762 785. Send your flight number, arrival date and number of passengers. We'll confirm your booking and your driver will be waiting at arrivals.
