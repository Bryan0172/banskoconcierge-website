What this is

A private-markets desk, not a listings feed.

Most of the property that matters at this level never reaches a portal. It moves quietly — between owners, developers and a small circle of people trusted to be discreet. BC Private Markets exists to give a qualified private buyer access to that circle in Bulgaria and Northern Greece, and the judgement to act on it well.

We work only for the buyer. We define your mandate, source and verify opportunities that fit it, run engineer-led due diligence on condition and structure, and coordinate the acquisition through to completion with the right local professionals around you.

  • Curated off-market opportunities — sourced through a vetted local network, not scraped from listings
  • Engineer-led verification — condition, structure and real renovation cost, judged on the ground
  • Buyer-side only — we represent you, never the seller
  • Coordinated end to end — legal, structuring and local execution arranged around a single point of contact
Two clear offerings

Investor Support and Private Markets — where the line sits.

Both are buyer-side, and both are honest about what they are. The difference is orientation versus active representation — and we keep them cleanly separate so you always know which one you are engaging.

Verified-first

Discretion is a process, not a promise.

Off-market opportunities are entrusted to us in confidence. To protect the owners who share them — and to respect everyone's time — confidential detail is opened only to buyers who have confirmed a mandate and, at the appropriate stage, readiness of funds.

This is not a hurdle placed in front of you. It is the same standard a serious private office or prime buy-side desk applies, and it is precisely what makes genuine off-market access possible. You are verified so that the opportunities you see are real, and so that the owners behind them stay protected.

  • A written buyer mandate — what you are looking for, and on what terms
  • Readiness of funds confirmed at the appropriate stage — never uploaded through this website
  • Confidential opportunity detail released step by step, as trust is established
Fees & conflicts

You will always know who pays us, and for what.

Trust at this level is built on disclosure, not on hiding the economics. We work on two clearly separated bases, and we tell you which one applies before you engage — never both on the same mandate without your knowledge.

  • Advisory basis — we are engaged and paid by you, the buyer. Independent, no seller commission, our only interest is your outcome.
  • Network basis — where we introduce an opportunity held by a licensed local partner, a commission may be payable on completion. That commission, and who pays it, is disclosed to you before you proceed.

Where a transaction requires a regulated brokerage role, that role sits with licensed local partners who hold the listing or mandate. Our role is to represent you, verify what is in front of you, and coordinate — with the economics on the table.

Beyond the algorithm

What an algorithm can list, anyone can find.

A new generation of tools can scrape every portal in the region in seconds. That is genuinely useful — for listed stock, where the value is in speed and coverage. It also makes listed stock a commodity.

At this level, the edge is the opposite of coverage. It is access to what is never published, and the judgement to verify it before you commit — an owner who will only sell quietly, a building whose real condition sits behind a good photograph, a structure that needs local knowledge to hold up. That is where a person, on the ground, still decides the outcome.

Behind Private Markets is a vetted network of local prime partners — brokers, developers and owners in Bulgaria and Northern Greece — who bring us opportunities before they are advertised, and often instead of advertising them. Important boundaries

What Private Markets does not do

We provide access, verification and coordination for the buyer. We do not provide regulated financial, legal or tax services, and we do not make promises we cannot stand behind.

This service does not replace

Guaranteed rental yield projections or income forecasts. Residency or citizenship assurances. Tax advice on property income or capital gains. Licensed financial or investment advice. Legal due diligence or contract review by a qualified lawyer. Official valuation or structural certification. Where these are needed, we introduce licensed professionals with a track record we trust.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Is BC Private Markets an estate agency?
We act for the buyer, not the seller. BC Private Markets is independent buy-side advisory and curated access — we help a private investor define a mandate, source and verify opportunities and coordinate the acquisition. Where a transaction requires a regulated brokerage role, that sits with licensed local partners who hold the listing or mandate. Any commission payable on an introduction is disclosed to you before you engage.
How is this different from Investor Support?
Investor Support is independent local orientation for buyers exploring the market — no representation, no transaction role. BC Private Markets is a separate, active mandate: we represent a qualified buyer, open curated off-market opportunities and coordinate the acquisition end to end. Both are buyer-side; the difference is orientation versus active representation.
Why do you ask for a mandate and proof of funds before showing off-market opportunities?
Off-market opportunities are shared with us in confidence by owners and partners. To protect them — and to use everyone's time well — we open confidential detail only to buyers who have confirmed a mandate and, at the appropriate stage, readiness of funds. Verification is not a hurdle; it is the discretion that makes off-market access possible. Proof of funds is never submitted through this website.
Do you guarantee returns, residency or tax outcomes?
No. We do not provide guaranteed yield projections, residency assurances or tax advice. We provide access, verification and coordination; regulated financial, legal and tax matters are handled by licensed professionals we introduce.
Which regions do you cover?
Our focus for 2026–27 is deliberately narrow: Bulgaria — with a base in Bansko and Razlog — and Northern Greece. Selected Balkan markets we cover through established local partners rather than claiming our own desk there. If your interest sits close to but outside this footprint, tell us; we would rather be straight about fit than force one.
