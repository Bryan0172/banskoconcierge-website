Questions & Answers

Private Mobility — Common Questions

What is private mobility coordination and who is it for?
Private mobility coordination means we arrange trusted drivers, vehicles and routing on your behalf — so you don't need to search, negotiate or manage logistics yourself. It is used by property owners visiting Bulgaria, investors on viewing trips, families relocating and private clients who need reliable, discreet movement through the region.
Can you arrange multi-stop viewing routes across Bulgaria?
Yes. Multi-stop investor viewing routes are a core use case. We coordinate drivers and routing across multiple cities — Sofia, Bansko, Plovdiv, Varna, the coast, the mountains — depending on properties being visited. Routes can run over one or several days.
Do you cover cross-border routes into the wider Balkans?
Yes. We coordinate routes from Bulgaria into Greece, Serbia, North Macedonia, Romania and beyond. Cross-border routing requires advance notice to confirm drivers and logistics. Contact us with your route and dates.
How is this different from booking a taxi or using an app?
This is coordinated, not booked. We source trusted drivers we have worked with, confirm your route and timing, and stay available throughout. There is no app, no anonymous driver, no meter. You have a named contact managing the arrangement from beginning to end.
Can you arrange recurring mobility support for property owners who visit regularly?
Yes. Owners who visit their Bulgarian property several times a year use us as an ongoing mobility coordinator. We maintain driver relationships and routing preferences so each visit runs consistently without needing to re-explain requirements.
How do I arrange private mobility coordination?
Contact us via WhatsApp at +359 895 762 785. Share your travel dates, route and number of passengers. We confirm drivers, vehicles and logistics and stay available throughout your journey.
