What We Arrange

Three types of arrangement.
All sourced locally. All private.

Module 1
In-Residence Private Dining
A trusted local chef cooks in your villa, apartment or chalet. Menu agreed in advance, ingredients sourced fresh, full service in your residence. The kitchen is left as found. You and your guests focus entirely on the evening.
Available in Bansko, Sofia, Plovdiv, Black Sea properties and rural estates across Bulgaria.
Module 2
Regional Wine & Cuisine Access
Curated access to regional food and wine producers — Thracian Valley wineries, local cheesemakers, mountain herb distilleries. Arranged as private visits or as part of your stay, not as group tours.
Sourced through local relationships. Not available on booking platforms.
Module 3
Curated Local Experience Access
Authentic access to local settings, markets, craftspeople and cultural experiences that are not part of the standard visitor circuit. For guests who want to understand the region they are in — or investing in — rather than observe it from the outside.
Requires lead time. Discussed case by case based on location and interest.

Questions & Answers

Private Dining — Common Questions

What is in-residence private dining and how is it arranged?
In-residence private dining means a trusted local chef cooks in your villa, apartment or chalet — not in a restaurant. We source the chef, confirm menu preferences in advance, and coordinate the full arrangement. You receive a private dining experience without leaving your residence.
Is this available outside of Bansko, across Bulgaria?
Yes. We arrange in-residence dining across Bulgaria — Bansko, Sofia, Plovdiv, Black Sea properties and rural estates. In the wider Balkans, arrangements depend on location and lead time. Contact us with your location and dates.
What does curated local experience access mean in practice?
It means we connect you with local settings, producers and access that is not part of standard tourist arrangements — a wine producer in the Thracian Valley, a local market visit with a knowledgeable guide, an authentic regional meal in a setting not listed on booking platforms. The access is sourced through local relationships.
Is this suitable for property owners hosting guests at their Bulgarian property?
Yes. Owners who use their Bulgarian property to host family, business associates or private guests often use this to provide a considered in-residence dining experience. We handle all sourcing and coordination so the owner does not have to.
How far in advance should I arrange private dining?
We recommend at least 48 hours for standard in-residence dining arrangements. For curated experiences involving specific producers or access, 3-5 days is preferable. Last-minute requests are considered where logistics allow — contact us via WhatsApp.
Is there a SPA or wellness component to this service?
No. This service focuses entirely on in-residence dining and authentic local access. We do not arrange SPA treatments, massage or wellness programmes. If you need a different type of local execution support, see our Local Execution Support service.
