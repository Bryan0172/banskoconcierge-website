01

Property Risk Check Bulgaria

Before you buy — or if you have owned for years and want to understand what you are dealing with.

We assess visible building condition, moisture and mold risk, obvious construction defects and realistic renovation cost ranges.

Legal checks and seller information do not reveal damp walls, failing insulation, hidden defects or future renovation risk. You get a clearer picture of the real cost and risk before you commit.

Owner Care Bulgaria

You own property in Bulgaria. You are not here most of the year.

Reliable local presence: regular inspections, contractor coordination, maintenance follow-up, issue response and direct reporting.

Relocation Bulgaria Orientation

Relocating to Bulgaria — even partially — involves more than finding a place to live.

Practical orientation: location choice, everyday setup, banking, residency steps, local contacts, trusted professional referrals and how the system works in real life.

You arrive with a clearer plan and local support. The first months in Bulgaria become more structured and less chaotic.
