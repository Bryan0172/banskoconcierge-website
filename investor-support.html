What most buyers overlook

Property investment needs more than listings and promises.

Bulgaria attracts international buyers for clear reasons: relatively low entry prices, EU membership, a growing rental market in certain areas and a straightforward legal framework for foreign ownership. The opportunity is real. So is the gap between what agents present and what buyers actually find on the ground.

Most international investors arrive with a shortlist of properties and a set of assumptions — about renovation costs, rental yields, management complexity and local costs. Very few of those assumptions survive contact with the actual situation. The properties that look attractive in listings often carry hidden condition issues. The renovation costs that seem low are frequently underestimated by a factor of two or more.

Investor Support gives you access to local knowledge before you commit — not to make the decision for you, but to make sure the decision is based on the real picture.

After the purchase

What happens after the purchase matters.

Many investors focus entirely on the acquisition and underestimate the complexity of managing property in Bulgaria from abroad. Contractor coordination, seasonal maintenance, tenant oversight, condition monitoring — these require reliable local presence that most international owners do not have.

Our Owner Care service provides practical local oversight for investors who want their property looked after without managing every detail from another country.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What does Investor Support from Bansko Concierge include?
Practical local orientation for international buyers exploring property in Bulgaria. This includes local market context, visible property condition reviews, property-use scenario analysis, indicative renovation and operating cost estimates and introductions to trusted local professionals. It does not include investment advice, legal services or financial projections.
Can Bansko Concierge tell me what rental yield to expect from a Bulgarian property?
No. We do not provide rental yield projections or income forecasts — these depend on variables we cannot guarantee. What we can provide is honest local context about rental demand in specific areas, typical operating realities and the factors that influence performance.
What are the most common mistakes foreign investors make when buying property in Bulgaria?
Underestimating renovation costs is the most consistent issue — real local costs are frequently two or more times higher than initial estimates. Others include overestimating rental demand in specific areas, overlooking building condition, and not having reliable local contacts in place before the purchase completes.
How much does property renovation typically cost in Bulgaria?
Costs vary significantly by property type, condition and location. We can provide indicative cost orientation based on direct local experience, but we do not provide formal cost estimates or project guarantees. For larger renovation scopes, a proper site assessment is needed before any reliable cost range can be established.
Is Investor Support the same as working with a Bulgarian estate agent?
No. Estate agents represent the sale or rental of specific properties and are compensated on transaction. Investor Support is independent local orientation — we do not sell property, represent developers or have a stake in whether you buy or not. Our interest is in giving you an accurate picture, not in closing a deal.
What happens after the purchase if I need ongoing property oversight?
Our Owner Care service provides local property oversight for investors who need reliable presence on the ground — condition checks, issue response, contractor coordination and direct reporting. It is designed specifically for international owners who cannot be present regularly.
How do I get started with Investor Support from Bansko Concierge?
Contact us via WhatsApp or email with details about the properties you are considering, your goals and your timeline. We will confirm what local context we can provide and discuss the most useful starting point.
