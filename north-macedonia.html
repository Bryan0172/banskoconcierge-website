Questions & Answers

North Macedonia FAQ

What luxury concierge services are available in North Macedonia?
Bansko Concierge VIP offers private chef dining, VIP transfers from Skopje Airport, Lake Ohrid villa experiences, boat excursions, wine region tours and bespoke travel across North Macedonia.
Can I get a private chef in Skopje or Lake Ohrid?
Yes. We arrange private chefs for villas and apartments in both Skopje and at Lake Ohrid. Service starts per evening including shopping, cooking, service and clean-up.
How do I get a VIP transfer from Skopje Airport?
Contact us via WhatsApp. We arrange luxury transfers from Skopje Alexander the Great Airport to Skopje city centre, Lake Ohrid (2.5h) or directly to Bansko (3h).
How far is Lake Ohrid from Bansko?
Lake Ohrid is approximately 200 km from Bansko — about 2.5 hours by luxury vehicle. A popular same-day or overnight combination for guests staying in Bansko.
Is Lake Ohrid worth visiting for a luxury holiday?
Lake Ohrid is one of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes and a UNESCO World Heritage site. We curate private waterfront dining, villa stays, monastery tours and boat excursions across the lake.
